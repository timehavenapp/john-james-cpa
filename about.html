About John M. James

Licensed CPA and CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ with over three decades of professional experience serving Minnesota families and businesses

Your Trusted Financial Partner

For over 30 years, I have been providing comprehensive accounting and tax guidance to individuals, business owners, and corporations throughout Minnesota. My extensive experience across multiple client types gives me the breadth of knowledge needed to assist clients in virtually any financial situation.

I received my Bachelor of Accountancy from the University of Minnesota, Duluth, and hold both CPA and CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ certifications. This unique combination of credentials allows me to provide comprehensive financial services that extend far beyond traditional accounting, encompassing strategic tax planning, retirement planning, and estate planning.

My practice specializes in helping individuals and business owners with income tax and estate planning strategies, retirement planning for 401(k), SEP, and ESOP plans, as well as assisting client families navigate the complex challenges of aging parents, including healthcare decisions, living arrangements, and wealth transfer planning.

Personal Background & Community Involvement

I grew up in Maple Plain, Minnesota and currently reside in Medina with my wife, Lisa. We have one daughter and actively participate in church, community, and sporting events as a family. In my personal time, I enjoy playing hockey, fishing, and hunting. I am a proud member of the Orono Lions Club and regularly volunteer for community and charity opportunities throughout the Twin Cities area.
